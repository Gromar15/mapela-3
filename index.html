Our Strategy

Target Audience

We primarily target small tradespeople and DIY enthusiasts (B2C) — bricklayers, tilers, gardeners — who need a reliable bag that doesn't fall apart on the first use. Secondary audience: construction companies and property managers (B2B) for custom-order production.

Key Message

"Rubble belongs in a bag, not on the floor — and the bag has to last."
Mapela offers a Czech-made alternative to single-use plastic sacks: heavy impregnated cotton that handles up to 30 kg, resists water, and can be used over and over again.

Catchphrases & CTA

#MapelaStrong #CzechCraft #BagsBuiltToLast #BuildSmarter

CTA: "Order your bags →" · "Write to info@mapela.cz"

Why It Works

Customers know the frustration of a torn single-use bag. An emotional hook combined with concrete technical proof (canvas 280 g/m², 4 reinforced handles, 30 kg load capacity) convinces even the most sceptical buyer. The four-generation family story builds lasting trust.
